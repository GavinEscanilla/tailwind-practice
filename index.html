What are IEMS?

IEMs (In-Ear Monitors) are high-quality earbuds used by musicians, audio engineers, and audiophiles for precise sound monitoring. Unlike regular earbuds, IEMs fit deeper in the ear canal, providing better sound isolation and clearer audio details.
